January: Vanilla Month
In this month you will make a total of 21 rooms 
Week one will consist of planning aka choosing your regions
Week two will consist of 7 rooms you will be tasked with making rooms in the style of vanilla regions and they all have to be unique 
Week three will consist of 7 rooms same as week two but with msc 
Week four will consist of 7 rooms for watcher 
Week four is you will make a mega room for your favorite region ever 15-20
February: Modded Month 
In this month you will make a total of 28 rooms 
You will be tasked with making a room for any 28 modded regions region creators can not make rooms of there own region 
March: Prompt Generator Month
In this month you will make 31 rooms that all have to be one screeners
You will use one of the three prompt generators to make a room you have to do the bonus challenge and all the rooms much follow the prompts 
April: Massive Room Month
In this month you be tasked with making a 75 screener
Thourghout the month there will be small things to add but the month will have one big goal in mind to make a tall ass tower of your own design 
For the small goals
Week one: Drain 
Week two: Overgrown Area
Week three: Rotten Core
Week four: Temple
May: Tile Set Month 
In this month you be making 4 rooms
You will use the first days of weeks to create a tileset to use in your rooms a tile set is a minumum of 5-9-15 tiles the tiles you created must be the main focal point of your room and all 4 rooms must be one screeners
June: Prop Month 
In this month you will be 4 rooms with the last needing to be submitted on the 30th
In this month you will be making props to be the main focal point of your four rooms 
It can be multiple props or just one 
July: Material Month
In this month you will be making 2 rooms 
In this month you be tasked with making two different materials and two different rooms the materials must be the main focal point of the rooms
August: Effect Month 
In this month you will be making 4 rooms 
So for this month you be tasked with making four effects for your rooms and this must be the main focal point but heres the twist 
They need to be different types Plants Erosion Texture and wires
September: Creature Month 
In this month you be making 30 rooms 
So for this month you have to make rooms based around any creature and they have to be able to get around the room and if there hostile there has to be ways to dodge/avoid them/or fight them 
October: Free-for-All Month
In this month you will be making 31 rooms 
This month is a free for all you can make anything you want 
November: Fishswallower Month
In this month you will be making 16 rooms 
So for this month you be making rooms based of Fishswallower's Project Using her Level Editor for Reference she made 16 different regions a room has to be created for each region 
And finally December: Catch up Month
